Morteza Sabri

Bioinformatician combining genomics, transcriptomics, and data science to uncover biological insights. Seeking PhD opportunities.

About Me

I’m a biologist turned bioinformatician with a passion for translating complex omics datasets into actionable biological insights. Since 2017, I’ve developed and optimized pipelines for bulk and single-cell RNA sequencing, DNA methylation analysis, and genome assembly—combining hands-on wet-lab experience with advanced computational expertise. I thrive on collaborative projects that bridge experimental design and data-driven discovery, and I’m eager to advance my research through impactful PhD opportunities.
